Plastic Items to Avoid

Plastic Water Bottle

Use Stainless Steel Bottle instead.

Plastic Grocery Bag

Replace with Cloth Tote Bag.

Plastic Straw

Switch to Metal or Bamboo Straw.

Plastic Cutlery

Use Wooden or Metal Cutlery.

Plastic Food Wrap

Beeswax Wrap is a great option.

Plastic Cups

Opt for Reusable Cups.

Plastic Containers

Glass or Steel Containers work better.

Plastic Toothbrush

Choose a Bamboo Toothbrush.

Plastic Zip Bags

Use Silicone Reusable Bags.

Plastic Pens

Switch to refillable or bamboo pens.

Plastic Plates

Replace with metal or ceramic ones.

Plastic Shampoo Bottles

Try shampoo bars instead.

Plastic Packaging

Look for minimal or paper packaging.

Plastic Razors

Switch to safety razors.

Plastic Brushes

Use natural fiber brushes.

Plastic Laundry Jugs

Choose laundry sheets or refills.

Plastic Lunch Boxes

Go for stainless steel tiffins.

Plastic Toys

Prefer wooden or cloth toys.

Plastic Lighters

Use refillable lighters or matches.

Plastic Storage Drawers

Try wooden or bamboo alternatives.
